A quiet, clinical aesthetic.

SAGE EMR is built around restraint: a near-black ink for authority, healing teal for life, paper-cream for warmth. Typography pairs Fraunces (serif, for moments of gravity) with Geist (sans, for instrument-like clarity).

Voice

Calm in language. No exclamations, no marketing copy. "Patient registered" not "Success! Patient created!"

Patient-first phrasing. "Hassan's last visit" rather than "ID 4812 — last encounter".

Numbers do the talking. Tabular figures, full units, never abbreviated when ambiguity costs.

Errors are honest. "We can't reach NHIA right now — claim queued for retry" beats "Operation failed."
